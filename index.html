Mark Hutchinson

Hi! I’m a software developer living in Toronto, Canada. I develop games played by millions of people at Zynga. I’ve been described as curious, empathetic and hardworking.

My wife Katie is the executive director of VIBE Arts, a charitable organization dedicated to providing children and youth in under-resourced communities with arts education. We have an adorable dog named Stuckey.

I studied Computer Science at the University of Waterloo where I was in the co-operative education program. This gave me the opportunity to have internships at a variety of different companies like IBM and Blackberry. After graduating I worked at IBM for 3 years before moving to San Francisco to start at Zynga. In 2014 I relocated back to Toronto to work at Zynga Toronto. We're always growing so reach out to me if you want to join!

I love travelling, getting outside, learning new things, urban design, craft beer, and strong coffee.
